# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Nerio is a professional, inclusive project space. Participants are expected to communicate clearly, critique ideas rather than people, respect different levels of experience, and keep discussions focused on improving the project.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, discrimination, threats, deliberate disruption, publishing private information, and conduct that makes collaboration unsafe or hostile.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and comments that violate this standard. Repeated or severe violations may lead to a temporary or permanent ban from project spaces.

To report a concern privately, contact the maintainers through the GitHub profile of [@vpavlov-me](https://github.com/vpavlov-me). Reports will be handled discreetly.
